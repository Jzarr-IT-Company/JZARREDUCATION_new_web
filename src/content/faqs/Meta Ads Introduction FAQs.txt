1. What are Meta Ads?

Meta Ads are online advertisements run on platforms owned by Meta Platforms such as Facebook and Instagram.

2. Why are Meta Ads important for businesses?

Meta Ads allow businesses to reach targeted audiences, generate leads, and increase product sales.

3. Which platforms support Meta Ads?

Meta Ads run across Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and the Meta Audience Network.

4. What is Meta Ads Manager?

Meta Ads Manager is the platform used to create, manage, and analyze advertising campaigns.

5. Can beginners learn Meta Ads easily?

Yes, Meta Ads tools are beginner-friendly and widely used in digital marketing.

6. What types of businesses can use Meta Ads?

Businesses of all sizes can use Meta Ads for marketing and promotion.

7. Are Meta Ads suitable for small businesses?

Yes, small businesses often use Meta Ads because they allow flexible budgets.

8. What are campaign objectives in Meta Ads?

Campaign objectives define the goal of an ad such as traffic, leads, or sales.

9. What types of ads can be created?

Advertisers can create image ads, video ads, carousel ads, and story ads.

10. How do Meta Ads reach the right audience?

Meta uses advanced targeting options based on interests, behaviors, and demographics.

11. Do Meta Ads require a Facebook page?

Yes, most ads require a connected Facebook page or Instagram account.

12. Can Meta Ads help increase website traffic?

Yes, many businesses run ads specifically to drive traffic to their websites.

13. What skills will students learn in this module?

Students learn Meta advertising fundamentals and campaign strategy.

14. Are Meta Ads measurable?

Yes, performance metrics such as clicks, impressions, and conversions can be tracked.

15. Why are Meta Ads popular in digital marketing?

They offer powerful targeting and a large global audience.
