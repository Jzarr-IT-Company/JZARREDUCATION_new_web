1. What is Shopify?

Shopify is an e-commerce platform that allows individuals and businesses to create online stores and sell products.

2. How does the Shopify business model work?

Shopify provides tools for building an online store, managing products, processing payments, and fulfilling orders.

3. Do I need coding skills to use Shopify?

No, Shopify allows users to create stores using themes and drag-and-drop customization tools.

4. Is Shopify suitable for beginners?

Yes, Shopify is designed to be beginner-friendly and easy to use.

5. Can Shopify be used for dropshipping?

Yes, many entrepreneurs use Shopify to run dropshipping businesses.

6. What types of products can be sold on Shopify?

Businesses can sell physical products, digital products, and services.

7. Is Shopify a global platform?

Yes, Shopify stores can sell products to customers worldwide.

8. Do I need hosting for Shopify?

No, Shopify provides hosting as part of its platform.

9. Can Shopify be used for small businesses?

Yes, Shopify is widely used by startups and small businesses.

10. What makes Shopify different from marketplaces?

Shopify allows sellers to create their own branded store instead of selling within a marketplace.

11. Is Shopify good for e-commerce beginners?

Yes, it offers simple tools and tutorials for beginners.

12. Can Shopify integrate with other tools?

Yes, Shopify integrates with marketing, payment, and shipping tools.

13. Can I customize my Shopify store?

Yes, themes and apps allow customization.

14. What will students learn in this module?

Students learn the fundamentals of Shopify and how online stores operate.

15. Why do many businesses use Shopify?

Because it provides a complete e-commerce solution with simple setup
