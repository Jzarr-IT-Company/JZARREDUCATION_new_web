1. What is product listing in Shopify?

Product listing means adding products to your store with titles, descriptions, and images.

2. What information should a product listing include?

Title, description, price, images, and product variants.

3. How many images should a product have?

Multiple high-quality images improve customer trust.

4. What are product variants?

Variants allow different sizes, colors, or styles for one product.

5. Can digital products be listed?

Yes, Shopify supports digital product sales.

6. How important are product descriptions?

Descriptions explain product benefits and details.

7. What is SKU in Shopify?

SKU is a unique identifier used to track inventory.

8. Can products be organized into collections?

Yes, collections help categorize products.

9. Do product images affect sales?

Yes, high-quality images improve conversion rates.

10. What is inventory management?

Inventory management tracks product stock levels.

11. Can I edit product details later?

Yes, product listings can be updated anytime.

12. What are product tags?

Tags help organize and filter products.

13. Can videos be added to product pages?

Yes, videos improve product presentation.

14. Can I duplicate product listings?

Yes, duplication helps speed up listing similar items.

15. What skills will students learn?

Students learn product listing and store catalog management.
