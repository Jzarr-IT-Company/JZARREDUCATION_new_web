1. What is Meta Ads optimization?

Optimization is the process of improving ad performance by adjusting targeting, budget, and creatives.

2. Why is optimization important?

It helps increase conversions while reducing advertising costs.

3. What metrics should advertisers track?

Common metrics include CTR, CPC, conversions, and ROAS.

4. What is CTR in advertising?

CTR (Click-Through Rate) measures how many users click on an ad.

5. What is CPC?

CPC (Cost Per Click) measures how much advertisers pay for each click.

6. What is ROAS?

ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) measures the revenue generated from ads.

7. How can advertisers improve ad performance?

Performance can improve by testing different creatives, audiences, and budgets.

8. What is A/B testing?

A/B testing compares different ads to see which performs better.

9. What is audience retargeting?

Retargeting shows ads to users who previously interacted with your website or content.

10. What is lookalike audience targeting?

Lookalike audiences help advertisers reach people similar to existing customers.

11. How can campaigns be scaled?

Scaling can be done by increasing budgets or expanding audience targeting.

12. What is ad fatigue?

Ad fatigue occurs when the audience sees the same ad repeatedly.

13. How can advertisers refresh ads?

Advertisers can update creatives, headlines, and targeting.

14. How often should campaigns be optimized?

Campaigns should be reviewed regularly to maintain performance.

15. What skills will students gain?

Students learn campaign optimization, analytics interpretation, and scaling strategies.
