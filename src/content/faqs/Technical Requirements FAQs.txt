1. Do I need a laptop to attend the courses?

Yes, most courses require a laptop or computer so students can practice the skills taught during the training sessions.

2. Can I attend classes using a mobile phone only?

While you may be able to watch classes on a mobile phone, a laptop or desktop computer is recommended for completing practical tasks and assignments.

3. What are the minimum laptop requirements for the courses?

A laptop with at least 4 GB RAM, a modern web browser, and a stable internet connection is generally sufficient for most courses.

4. Which operating systems are supported?

Most courses can be completed using laptops running Windows, macOS, or other modern operating systems.

5. Do I need to install any specific software?

Depending on the course, students may need to install certain tools such as design software, browsers, or other digital platforms used during training.

6. Will the instructor guide us on software installation?

Yes, instructors usually guide students on how to install and use the required software during the course.

7. What internet speed is recommended for online classes?

A stable internet connection with at least 5–10 Mbps speed is recommended for smooth participation in online classes.

8. Do I need headphones or a microphone?

Headphones or earphones are recommended for better audio during online classes, and a microphone can help when interacting with instructors.

9. Is a webcam required for online classes?

A webcam is optional, but it may be helpful during interactive sessions or discussions.

10. Can I use a shared or office computer for the course?

Yes, you can use any computer that meets the technical requirements and allows you to install or access the necessary software.

11. Will I need a high-performance laptop for these courses?

Most beginner-level courses do not require high-end laptops. A standard laptop capable of running modern software should be sufficient.

12. What browser should I use for online classes and course tools?

Modern browsers such as Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, or Mozilla Firefox are generally recommended for the best experience.

13. Do I need additional storage on my laptop?

Yes, having sufficient storage space is helpful for saving project files, course materials, and assignments.

14. What should I do if my system does not meet the requirements?

If your system does not meet the requirements, you may consider upgrading your device or consulting the support team for guidance.

15. Who can I contact for technical setup help?

If you need assistance with technical setup, you can contact the support team through the contact details available on jzarreducation.com.
