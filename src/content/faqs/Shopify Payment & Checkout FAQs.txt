1. What payment methods does Shopify support?

Shopify supports credit cards, digital wallets, and online payment gateways.

2. What is Shopify Payments?

Shopify Payments is Shopify’s built-in payment processing system.

3. Can third-party payment gateways be used?

Yes, Shopify supports multiple payment gateways.

4. What is the checkout process?

Checkout is the process where customers complete purchases.

5. Are transactions secure?

Yes, Shopify uses secure payment technology.

6. Can customers pay in different currencies?

Yes, Shopify supports multi-currency payments.

7. What is abandoned checkout?

An abandoned checkout occurs when a customer leaves before completing payment.

8. Can abandoned carts be recovered?

Yes, Shopify allows automated cart recovery emails.

9. Are there transaction fees?

Fees may apply depending on payment gateway selection.

10. Can payment methods be customized?

Yes, store owners can enable or disable payment options.

11. What is Shopify invoice billing?

It is the billing system for Shopify subscription fees.

12. Do digital products use the same checkout?

Yes, the checkout process is similar.

13. Can discount codes be applied during checkout?

Yes, Shopify supports discount and promo codes.

14. What happens after a successful payment?

The order is confirmed and processed.

15. What skills will students learn?

Students learn payment setup and checkout optimization.
