1. What is on-page SEO?

On-page SEO refers to optimizing individual pages of a website.

2. Why is on-page SEO important?

It helps search engines understand page content.

3. What elements are included in on-page SEO?

Title tags, meta descriptions, headings, content, and images.

4. What is a meta title?

A meta title is the clickable headline in search results.

5. What is a meta description?

A meta description summarizes webpage content.

6. What are heading tags?

Heading tags organize page content into sections.

7. What is internal linking?

Internal linking connects pages within the same website.

8. Why is content important for SEO?

Quality content helps answer user queries.

9. What is keyword placement?

Keywords should be placed naturally within content.

10. What is image optimization?

Optimizing images improves page speed and SEO.

11. What is URL optimization?

Clean and descriptive URLs improve rankings.

12. Can content updates improve rankings?

Yes, updated content can improve search visibility.

13. What is readability in SEO?

Content should be easy to understand for readers.

14. Can beginners perform on-page SEO?

Yes, many tools guide users through optimization.

15. What skills will students gain?

Students learn page optimization techniques.
