1. What is Upwork?

Upwork is a freelancing platform where businesses hire professionals for various online services.

2. How does Upwork work?

Clients post jobs and freelancers submit proposals to offer their services.

3. Who can work on Upwork?

Freelancers with skills in areas such as design, development, marketing, and writing can work on Upwork.

4. Is Upwork suitable for beginners?

Yes, beginners can start freelancing by building a strong profile and submitting proposals.

5. What services are popular on Upwork?

Services like web development, graphic design, digital marketing, and SEO are commonly offered.

6. Do freelancers need experience to start?

Experience helps, but beginners can start by building portfolios and learning skills.

7. What is a freelance contract on Upwork?

A contract is an agreement between a freelancer and a client for completing work.

8. What is hourly vs fixed-price work?

Hourly jobs pay based on time worked, while fixed-price jobs pay for completed projects.

9. How do freelancers receive payments?

Payments are processed through the Upwork platform after work approval.

10. What is a freelancer profile?

A profile shows your skills, experience, and services.

11. How do clients choose freelancers?

Clients review profiles, proposals, ratings, and portfolios.

12. What is a freelancer portfolio?

A portfolio displays previous work samples.

13. Can freelancers work with international clients?

Yes, Upwork connects freelancers with clients worldwide.

14. What skills will students learn in this module?

Students learn freelancing basics and how online marketplaces operate.

15. Why is Upwork popular among freelancers?

It provides access to global clients and remote work opportunities.
