Etsy is an online marketplace where people buy and sell handmade items, vintage goods, craft supplies, and digital products.

2. What type of products are sold on Etsy?

Products commonly sold include handmade crafts, art prints, digital downloads, jewelry, home decor, and vintage items.

3. How does the Etsy business model work?

Sellers create a shop, list products, and customers purchase items directly through the Etsy platform.

4. Is Etsy suitable for beginners?

Yes, Etsy is beginner-friendly and many new sellers start their online business through the platform.

5. Can digital products be sold on Etsy?

Yes, Etsy is one of the most popular platforms for selling digital products like templates, planners, and artwork.

6. Do I need inventory to sell on Etsy?

Not always. Many sellers offer digital downloads which require no physical inventory.

7. Is Etsy a global marketplace?

Yes, sellers can reach customers from different countries around the world.

8. What are Etsy seller fees?

Etsy charges listing fees, transaction fees, and payment processing fees.

9. Can I build a full business using Etsy?

Yes, many entrepreneurs build successful online businesses using Etsy.

10. What skills are needed to sell on Etsy?

Basic skills such as product design, marketing, SEO, and customer service are helpful.

11. What makes Etsy different from other marketplaces?

Etsy focuses mainly on handmade, creative, and unique products.

12. Is Etsy good for creative entrepreneurs?

Yes, Etsy is especially popular among artists, designers, and creative sellers.

13. Do Etsy sellers need branding?

Branding helps sellers stand out and build customer trust.

14. How do customers find products on Etsy?

Customers find products through Etsy search, recommendations, and external marketing.
