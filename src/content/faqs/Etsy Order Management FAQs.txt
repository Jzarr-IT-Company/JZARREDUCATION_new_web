1. What is order management on Etsy?

Order management involves tracking purchases, processing orders, and communicating with customers on Etsy.

2. How do I view my orders on Etsy?

Orders can be managed through the Etsy seller dashboard.

3. What happens when a customer places an order?

The seller receives a notification and prepares the product for delivery.

4. How are digital product orders handled?

Digital orders are automatically delivered to the buyer after payment.

5. How are physical product orders processed?

Sellers must pack and ship the product according to their shop policies.

6. Can sellers track order status?

Yes, the seller dashboard shows order progress and shipping details.

7. What happens if a customer cancels an order?

Sellers can process cancellations depending on their shop policies.

8. How do I communicate with customers about orders?

Customer messages can be handled through Etsy’s messaging system.

9. What should I do if an order issue occurs?

Sellers should resolve the issue promptly and communicate with the customer.

10. How are returns handled?

Return policies are determined by the shop’s policies.

11. Can sellers automate order notifications?

Yes, Etsy automatically sends order confirmations to buyers.

12. Why is order management important?

Efficient order management improves customer satisfaction.

13. How do sellers manage multiple orders?

Orders can be organized and tracked from the seller dashboard.

14. What is order fulfillment time?

Fulfillment time refers to how long it takes to prepare and ship an order.

15. What skills will students gain from this module?

Students learn order tracking, customer communication, and fulfillment processes.
