1. What payment methods does Etsy support?

Etsy supports various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment systems.

2. What are Etsy listing fees?

Etsy charges a small fee for listing each product on the platform.

3. What is the Etsy transaction fee?

A transaction fee is charged when a product is sold.

4. What are payment processing fees?

Payment processing fees cover the cost of handling online payments.

5. How do sellers receive payments?

Payments are transferred to the seller’s bank account through Etsy’s payment system.

6. How long does it take to receive Etsy payments?

Payment schedules depend on Etsy’s payout system and seller settings.

7. What currency can sellers use?

Sellers can choose their preferred shop currency.

8. Are fees deducted automatically?

Yes, Etsy automatically deducts applicable fees from sales revenue.

9. What is Etsy payment reserve?

In some cases, Etsy may hold a temporary reserve for new sellers.

10. Can sellers track their earnings?

Yes, the seller dashboard shows revenue and transaction history.

11. What is the Etsy billing statement?

It shows all charges, fees, and earnings for the shop.

12. Do digital products have the same fees?

Digital products are subject to similar transaction and listing fees.

13. How do refunds affect payments?

Refunds may reduce the seller’s balance depending on the situation.

14. Are taxes included in Etsy fees?

Tax policies depend on seller location and local regulations.

15. What will students learn in this module?

Students learn how Etsy payments, fees, and financial management work.
