1. What is a Meta Ads campaign?

A campaign is the overall advertising structure that defines the marketing objective.

2. What are the levels of Meta Ads structure?

Meta Ads structure includes campaign, ad set, and ad levels.

3. What is an ad set?

An ad set controls audience targeting, budget, and ad placements.

4. What is an ad in Meta Ads?

An ad is the creative content shown to the audience.

5. How do I create a campaign in Meta Ads Manager?

Campaigns are created in Meta Ads Manager by selecting an objective and configuring settings.

6. What types of campaign objectives are available?

Objectives include awareness, traffic, engagement, leads, app installs, and sales.

7. What is audience targeting?

Audience targeting allows advertisers to select who will see their ads.

8. Can I target specific locations?

Yes, advertisers can target users based on country, city, or region.

9. What are ad placements?

Placements determine where ads appear across Meta platforms.

10. What is the campaign budget?

The budget is the amount advertisers spend on their ads.

11. What is daily vs lifetime budget?

Daily budget is spent per day, while lifetime budget is spent over the campaign duration.

12. How long does campaign setup take?

A campaign can be created within minutes using Ads Manager.

13. Can beginners run campaigns successfully?

Yes, beginners can start with simple campaigns and optimize later.

14. What creative formats are supported?

Ads can include images, videos, carousels, and slideshows.

15. What skills will students gain?

Students learn campaign creation, targeting strategies, and ad setup.
