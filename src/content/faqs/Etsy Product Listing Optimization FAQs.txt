1. What is listing optimization?

It is the process of improving product listings to increase visibility and sales.

2. Why is listing optimization important?

Optimized listings appear higher in Etsy search results.

3. What elements are included in a listing?

Title, images, description, tags, and pricing.

4. What is an optimized product title?

A clear title containing relevant keywords.

5. How many images should a listing have?

Multiple high-quality images improve buyer confidence.

6. What should be included in the description?

Product details, benefits, and usage instructions.

7. Why are tags important?

Tags help Etsy understand and rank your product.

8. What are listing attributes?

Attributes describe product characteristics like color or material.

9. Do images affect conversion rates?

Yes, professional images significantly increase sales.

10. What is conversion optimization?

Improving listings to convert visitors into buyers.

11. How often should listings be updated?

Regular updates can improve visibility.

12. Can optimized listings increase traffic?

Yes, better optimization improves search ranking.

13. Should listings include keywords?

Yes, relevant keywords help customers find products.

14. Do product videos help?

Yes, videos improve product presentation.

15. What skills will students gain?

Students learn listing creation, optimization, and product presentation.
