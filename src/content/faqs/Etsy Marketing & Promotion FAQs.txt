1. What is Etsy marketing?

Etsy marketing involves promoting products to increase traffic and sales on Etsy.

2. Why is marketing important for Etsy shops?

Marketing helps attract customers and increase product visibility.

3. What are Etsy Ads?

Etsy Ads allow sellers to promote their listings directly within the Etsy platform.

4. Can social media help promote Etsy shops?

Yes, platforms like Instagram, Pinterest, and Facebook can drive traffic to Etsy listings.

5. What is Etsy Offsite Ads?

Offsite Ads promote Etsy products on external platforms such as search engines and social media.

6. How do discounts help increase sales?

Discounts and promotional offers encourage customers to make purchases.

7. What is Etsy shop branding?

Branding involves creating a unique shop identity through logos, colors, and product presentation.

8. Do product photos affect marketing?

Yes, attractive product photos improve customer engagement.

9. Can influencers promote Etsy products?

Yes, influencers can help promote products to their audiences.

10. What is email marketing for Etsy shops?

Email marketing helps sellers build relationships with customers.

11. Do seasonal promotions increase sales?

Yes, many Etsy sellers benefit from seasonal marketing strategies.

12. What role does SEO play in marketing?

SEO helps products appear in search results and attract buyers.

13. Can content marketing help Etsy sellers?

Yes, blogs and social content can increase brand visibility.

14. How often should sellers promote their products?

Consistent promotion helps maintain visibility.

15. What skills will students learn in this module?

Students learn marketing strategies, promotion techniques, and audience growth.
