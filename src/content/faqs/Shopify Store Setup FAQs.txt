1. How do I create a Shopify store?

You can create a store by signing up on the Shopify website and following the store setup process.

2. What information is needed to create a store?

Basic details such as store name, email address, and business information are required.

3. Can beginners set up a Shopify store easily?

Yes, Shopify provides guided steps for setting up a store.

4. What is a Shopify theme?

A theme controls the design and layout of your online store.

5. Can I customize my Shopify theme?

Yes, Shopify themes can be customized using built-in settings.

6. Do I need a domain for my store?

Yes, you can use a Shopify domain or connect your own domain.

7. What are Shopify apps?

Apps extend store functionality such as marketing, analytics, and shipping tools.

8. How long does store setup take?

Basic store setup can be completed within a few hours.

9. Can I create multiple pages in my store?

Yes, you can create pages such as About Us, Contact, and Policies.

10. What is the Shopify dashboard?

The dashboard is where store owners manage products, orders, and settings.

11. Can I edit my store design anytime?

Yes, store design can be updated anytime.

12. Is mobile optimization included?

Yes, Shopify themes are usually mobile responsive.

13. Can Shopify stores sell internationally?

Yes, Shopify supports international selling.

14. What is store branding?

Branding includes logos, colors, and visual identity.

15. What skills will students learn?

Students learn how to build and configure a professional Shopify store.
