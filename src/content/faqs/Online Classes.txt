1. How are online classes conducted at Jzarr Education?

Online classes at Jzarr Education are conducted through Zoho Meeting, where students can attend live sessions, watch demonstrations, and interact with instructors in real time.

2. Which platform is used for online classes?

All online classes are conducted using Zoho Meeting, a professional online meeting platform that allows live video training and screen sharing.

3. Do I need to install any software to attend online classes?

Yes, students may need to install the Zoho Meeting application or join directly through their web browser using the meeting link provided by the instructor.

4. How will I receive the online class link?

Students usually receive the class link through email, WhatsApp, or the official communication channel before the scheduled class time.

5. What equipment do I need for online classes?

You need a computer or laptop, stable internet connection, headphones or speakers, and optionally a microphone to participate in discussions.

6. Can I attend online classes from my mobile phone?

Yes, students can join online classes using a smartphone by installing the Zoho Meeting mobile app.

7. Are online classes live or recorded?

Most classes are conducted live so students can interact with instructors and ask questions during the session.

8. Can I ask questions during the online class?

Yes, students can ask questions during the class using voice, chat, or the question feature available in Zoho Meeting.

9. What should I do if I miss an online class?

If you miss a class, you should contact the instructor or support team to check if the session recording or additional guidance is available.

10. Is there a fixed schedule for online classes?

Yes, online classes are conducted according to a predefined schedule shared with students after enrollment.

11. How long does each online class session last?

Class duration may vary depending on the course, but most sessions typically last between 1 to 2 hours.

12. Do I need a strong internet connection for online classes?

Yes, a stable internet connection is recommended to ensure smooth video streaming and participation in live sessions.

13. Can I interact with other students during the class?

Yes, students can communicate with instructors and fellow learners through chat or discussion features during the session.

14. Are online classes suitable for beginners?

Yes, online classes are designed to be beginner-friendly, with step-by-step explanations and practical demonstrations.

15. Who should I contact if I have technical issues joining the class?

If you face technical issues joining the session, you can contact the support team or instructor for immediate assistance.
