1. What are e-commerce ads on Meta platforms?

E-commerce ads are advertisements created to promote online products and drive sales through platforms like Facebook and Instagram.

2. Why are Meta Ads important for e-commerce businesses?

Meta Ads help online stores reach targeted audiences, increase website traffic, and generate product sales.

3. What types of e-commerce ads can be created?

Advertisers can create image ads, video ads, carousel ads, collection ads, and dynamic product ads.

4. What is the main objective of e-commerce advertising?

The main goal is to drive product purchases and increase revenue for an online store.

5. Can Meta Ads be used for Shopify or website stores?

Yes, Meta Ads can promote products from any e-commerce website such as Shopify or WooCommerce stores.

6. What is a product catalog in Meta Ads?

A product catalog contains a list of products from an online store that can be used to create dynamic ads.

7. What are dynamic product ads?

Dynamic ads automatically show relevant products to users based on their browsing behavior.

8. What is retargeting in e-commerce advertising?

Retargeting allows advertisers to show ads to people who previously visited their website or viewed products.

9. What is the role of the Meta Pixel in e-commerce ads?

The Meta Pixel tracks website visitor behavior and helps measure ad performance and conversions.

10. How do e-commerce ads target the right audience?

Advertisers can target users based on demographics, interests, behavior, and previous interactions.

11. What budget is required to start e-commerce ads?

Businesses can start with small budgets and scale campaigns based on performance.

12. How can product images improve ad performance?

High-quality images attract attention and increase the likelihood of clicks and purchases.

13. What is a conversion in e-commerce advertising?

A conversion occurs when a user completes a desired action, such as purchasing a product.

14. Can beginners run e-commerce ads?

Yes, beginners can start with basic campaigns and optimize them as they gain experience.

15. What skills will students gain from this module?

Students learn product promotion strategies, campaign setup, retargeting techniques, and performance optimization for online stores.
