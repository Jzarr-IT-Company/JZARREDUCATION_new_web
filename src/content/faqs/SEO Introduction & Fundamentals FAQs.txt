1. What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of improving a website so it appears higher in search engine results.

2. Why is SEO important for websites?

SEO helps websites attract organic traffic from search engines like Google.

3. What is organic traffic?

Organic traffic refers to visitors who find a website through unpaid search results.

4. Can beginners learn SEO easily?

Yes, SEO basics can be learned step by step with practice.

5. What types of businesses benefit from SEO?

Almost all businesses with websites benefit from SEO.

6. How long does SEO take to show results?

SEO results may take weeks or months depending on competition and strategy.

7. What are search engine rankings?

Rankings represent the position of a website in search results.

8. What is a search engine algorithm?

Algorithms determine how search engines rank websites.

9. What is a keyword in SEO?

A keyword is a search term people type into search engines.

10. What is SEO strategy?

An SEO strategy is a plan to improve website visibility.

11. Can SEO increase business sales?

Yes, higher search rankings can lead to more traffic and customers.

12. Is SEO better than paid ads?

SEO provides long-term traffic while ads provide immediate visibility.

13. Do all websites need SEO?

Yes, SEO helps websites reach their target audience.

14. What will students learn in this module?

Students learn SEO basics, search engines, and ranking principles.

15. Why is SEO a valuable digital skill?

SEO is widely used in digital marketing and online businesses.
