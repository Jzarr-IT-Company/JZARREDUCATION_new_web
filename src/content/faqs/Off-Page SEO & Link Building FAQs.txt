1. What is off-page SEO?

Off-page SEO focuses on actions taken outside a website to improve rankings.

2. What is link building?

Link building is the process of getting other websites to link to your site.

3. Why are backlinks important?

Backlinks signal trust and authority to search engines.

4. What is a high-quality backlink?

A backlink from a reputable website.

5. What is guest posting?

Guest posting involves writing articles for other websites.

6. What is domain authority?

Domain authority measures a website’s credibility.

7. Can social media influence SEO?

Social signals may indirectly help SEO visibility.

8. What is anchor text?

Anchor text is the clickable text in a hyperlink.

9. What is natural link building?

Links earned through valuable content.

10. What are toxic backlinks?

Low-quality or spam links that harm rankings.

11. How can backlinks be analyzed?

SEO tools analyze link profiles.

12. Can beginners build backlinks?

Yes, through outreach and content marketing.

13. What is brand mention in SEO?

Brand mentions increase online visibility.

14. How long does link building take?

It is a gradual long-term process.

15. What skills will students gain?

Students learn backlink strategies and outreach methods.
