1. What is Amazon PPC advertising?

Amazon PPC (Pay-Per-Click) advertising allows sellers to promote their products by paying when shoppers click on their ads.

2. Why is PPC important for Amazon sellers?

PPC helps increase product visibility, generate traffic, and boost product sales.

3. What types of PPC ads exist on Amazon?

Amazon offers different ad types including Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands, and Sponsored Display ads.

4. What will I learn in the Amazon PPC module?

Students learn campaign setup, keyword targeting, bidding strategies, and performance optimization.

5. What is keyword targeting in PPC?

Keyword targeting allows ads to appear when customers search for specific product-related terms.

6. What is automatic PPC campaign targeting?

Automatic campaigns allow Amazon to choose keywords based on product relevance.

7. What is manual PPC targeting?

Manual campaigns allow sellers to choose specific keywords and bidding strategies.

8. How does PPC affect product ranking?

Well-managed PPC campaigns can improve product visibility and support organic ranking.

9. How do sellers control PPC costs?

Sellers control costs by setting daily budgets and adjusting keyword bids.

10. What is ACoS in Amazon advertising?

ACoS (Advertising Cost of Sales) measures the efficiency of an advertising campaign.

11. Can beginners run Amazon PPC campaigns?

Yes, beginners can start with simple campaigns and optimize them over time.

12. How long should a PPC campaign run?

Campaign duration depends on strategy, product performance, and advertising goals.

13. What are negative keywords in PPC?

Negative keywords prevent ads from showing for irrelevant search terms.

14. Will I learn how to optimize PPC campaigns?

Yes, students learn strategies for improving campaign performance and reducing wasted ad spend.

15. What skills will I gain from this module?

Students learn advertising strategy, keyword analysis, campaign optimization, and performance tracking.
