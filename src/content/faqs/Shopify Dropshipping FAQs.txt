1. What is Shopify dropshipping?

Shopify dropshipping is an e-commerce business model where sellers list products in their store without holding inventory. When a customer places an order, the supplier ships the product directly to the customer.

2. How does dropshipping work on Shopify?

Sellers create a store using Shopify, add products from suppliers, and when orders are placed, the supplier handles shipping and fulfillment.

3. Do I need inventory for dropshipping?

No, dropshipping allows you to sell products without keeping inventory.

4. What platforms help with Shopify dropshipping?

Tools like DSers and marketplaces like AliExpress are commonly used to source products.

5. Is dropshipping suitable for beginners?

Yes, dropshipping is considered beginner-friendly because it requires low startup investment.

6. How do I choose products for dropshipping?

Product research focuses on demand, competition, and profit margin.

7. Do I need a supplier for dropshipping?

Yes, suppliers are responsible for storing and shipping the products.

8. What are the advantages of dropshipping?

Advantages include low startup cost, no inventory management, and flexible product selection.

9. Are there risks in dropshipping?

Challenges may include supplier reliability, shipping delays, and product quality issues.

10. Can Shopify automate dropshipping orders?

Yes, many apps can automate order processing and product imports.

11. What is product sourcing in dropshipping?

It involves finding reliable suppliers who provide products for your store.

12. How do sellers manage customer service?

Even though suppliers ship products, store owners handle customer communication.

13. What role does marketing play in dropshipping?

Marketing helps drive traffic and convert visitors into customers.

14. Can dropshipping be scaled into a large business?

Yes, many successful e-commerce brands started with dropshipping.

15. What skills will students learn in this module?

Students learn supplier sourcing, product research, order automation, and dropshipping strategies.
