1. What is Amazon FBA?

Amazon FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon) is a service where sellers send their products to Amazon warehouses, and Amazon handles storage, packing, shipping, and customer service.

2. What is Amazon FBM?

Amazon FBM (Fulfillment by Merchant) means the seller manages product storage, packing, shipping, and customer service themselves instead of Amazon handling it.

3. What is the difference between FBA and FBM?

In FBA, Amazon manages logistics and customer service, while in FBM the seller handles all fulfillment tasks independently.

4. Which fulfillment method is better for beginners?

Many beginners prefer FBA because Amazon handles shipping and logistics, making the selling process easier.

5. Do I need a warehouse for FBM?

Yes, FBM sellers usually store and ship products from their own location or warehouse.

6. What are the advantages of Amazon FBA?

FBA offers faster shipping, Amazon Prime eligibility, and Amazon-managed customer support.

7. Are there fees for Amazon FBA?

Yes, Amazon charges fulfillment, storage, and service fees for using FBA.

8. Can I switch between FBA and FBM?

Yes, sellers can choose between FBA and FBM depending on their business strategy.

9. Does Amazon handle product returns in FBA?

Yes, Amazon manages customer returns and refunds for FBA orders.

10. What products are suitable for FBA?

Products with consistent demand, manageable size, and good profit margins often work well with FBA.

11. Can FBM sellers still succeed on Amazon?

Yes, many sellers use FBM successfully, especially when managing their own logistics efficiently.

12. How do I send products to Amazon warehouses?

Products must be prepared and shipped according to Amazon's packaging and labeling requirements.

13. Do FBA products get better visibility?

FBA listings may benefit from Prime eligibility, which can attract more customers.

14. Will I learn both FBA and FBM strategies in this module?

Yes, this module explains both fulfillment methods so students can choose the best option.

15. What skills will I gain from this module?

Students learn logistics management, fulfillment strategies, and operational processes for Amazon selling.
