1. What is E-commerce SEO?

E-commerce SEO focuses on optimizing online store pages to rank higher in search engines.

2. Why is SEO important for online stores?

SEO helps e-commerce websites attract organic traffic and increase product sales.

3. What pages should be optimized for e-commerce SEO?

Product pages, category pages, and blog content.

4. What are product keywords?

Keywords customers use when searching for products online.

5. Why are product descriptions important?

Detailed descriptions improve search visibility and customer understanding.

6. What is product schema markup?

Schema markup helps search engines display product details in search results.

7. How do product reviews affect SEO?

Reviews add fresh content and increase customer trust.

8. What is category page optimization?

Improving category pages with keywords and structured content.

9. What is internal linking in e-commerce?

Linking product pages and categories within the website.

10. Why is site structure important for e-commerce SEO?

A clear structure helps search engines crawl product pages.

11. What is duplicate content in e-commerce?

Duplicate content occurs when similar product descriptions appear on multiple pages.

12. How important are product images for SEO?

Optimized images improve user experience and page speed.

13. Can e-commerce SEO increase conversions?

Yes, better rankings lead to more visitors and potential buyers.

14. What is mobile SEO for e-commerce?

Optimizing online stores for mobile devices.

15. What skills will students learn?

Students learn product optimization and online store SEO strategies.
