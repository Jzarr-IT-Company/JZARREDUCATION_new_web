1. What is product research for Etsy?

Product research helps identify profitable products with high demand.

2. Why is product research important?

It helps sellers choose products that customers are actively searching for.

3. What factors should be analyzed?

Demand, competition, pricing, and market trends.

4. What tools can help with research?

Tools like eRank and Marmalead help analyze Etsy market data.

5. How do I find trending products?

By analyzing Etsy search results and trending categories.

6. What is a niche in Etsy selling?

A niche is a specific product category or target audience.

7. Should beginners focus on a niche?

Yes, niche targeting helps reduce competition.

8. Can digital products be researched?

Yes, digital product trends can be analyzed using keyword tools.

9. How important are customer reviews in research?

Reviews reveal customer needs and product improvement ideas.

10. What is competition analysis?

Evaluating the number of sellers and product listings in a niche.

11. What is a profitable Etsy product?

A product with strong demand, manageable competition, and good margins.

12. How long should research take?

Proper research may take several hours or days.

13. Can product research reduce risk?

Yes, research helps avoid saturated markets.

14. Do seasonal trends matter?

Yes, many Etsy products are seasonal.

15. What will students learn in this module?

Students learn how to find profitable product ideas.
