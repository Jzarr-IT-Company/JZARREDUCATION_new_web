1. What are digital products on Etsy?

Digital products are downloadable files that customers can purchase and download instantly from Etsy without physical shipping.

2. What types of digital products sell well on Etsy?

Popular digital products include planners, templates, printable art, invitations, social media templates, and digital guides.

3. Do I need inventory to sell digital products?

No, digital products require no physical inventory because customers download them after purchase.

4. What file formats are commonly used for digital products?

Common formats include PDF, PNG, JPG, SVG, and ZIP files.

5. Can beginners sell digital products on Etsy?

Yes, many beginners start their Etsy business by selling digital downloads.

6. Do digital products require shipping?

No, digital downloads are delivered automatically to customers through Etsy.

7. What tools can help create digital products?

Tools like Canva, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe Illustrator are commonly used.

8. How do customers receive digital downloads?

After purchase, customers can download files directly from their Etsy account.

9. Can digital products be sold multiple times?

Yes, digital products can be sold unlimited times since they are not physical items.

10. Are digital products profitable on Etsy?

Yes, digital products can be highly profitable due to low production and delivery costs.

11. Do digital listings require clear instructions?

Yes, providing clear usage instructions improves customer satisfaction.

12. Can digital products be customized?

Some sellers offer customizable digital products depending on the product type.

13. What is the advantage of selling digital products?

Digital products require no shipping, inventory, or storage.

14. How important are product previews?

Preview images help customers understand what they will receive.

15. What skills will students learn in this module?

Students learn how to create, list, and sell digital products successfully.
