1. What is an Etsy shop?

An Etsy shop is your online store where you display and sell products.

2. How do I create my Etsy shop?

After creating an account, you can set up your shop by selecting a shop name and adding product listings.

3. How important is the shop name?

A unique and memorable shop name helps build brand recognition.

4. Can I change my shop name later?

Yes, Etsy allows limited shop name changes.

5. What information should be added to the shop profile?

Shop description, banner image, policies, and seller information.

6. Why is shop branding important?

Branding helps build trust and attract customers.

7. What are shop policies?

Policies explain shipping, returns, and customer service terms.

8. Can I customize my shop appearance?

Yes, Etsy allows customization of shop banners, logos, and descriptions.

9. Do I need a logo for my shop?

A logo helps create a professional brand identity.

10. How many products should I list initially?

Many experts recommend listing multiple products to increase visibility.

11. Can I sell multiple product categories?

Yes, sellers can offer different product types within their shop.

12. How do customers discover Etsy shops?

Through Etsy search results and recommendations.

13. What are shop sections?

Sections help organize products into categories.

14. Does shop design affect sales?

Yes, a professional shop design improves customer trust.

15. What will students learn in this module?

Students learn how to build and customize a professional Etsy store.
